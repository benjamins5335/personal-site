Overview

Wedding celebrations will take place over two days, including a welcome ceilidh on Friday the 7th of November for those who can make it. This will be followed by our wedding and reception on Saturday the 8th of November. Let us know in the RSVP form if you plan on making both!

Transportation to the Church and then the reception venue on the day of the wedding is available via coach and will be departing from the town of Beauly. Just let us know if you'd like to reserve a seat!

welcome ceilidh

Friday, 7th of November
Moniack Castle, IV5 7PQ
6:30 PM - 10 PM
Dress Code: Casual
For those traveling from out of town but very much optional! A great opportunity to learn some reels/ceilidh dancing and meet other guests. Like traditional ceilidhs, there will be an open floor for guests to contribute words, music or any special talents!

wedding ceremony

Saturday, 8th of November
St. Mary's Church, Eskadale, IV4 4PJ

Dress Code: Unapologetic Glamour! Black Tie/Tartan encouraged.
The church might be cold so wrap up warm. Parking will be available in an adjacent field but there will be a bus service available from Beauly and to the reception after.
