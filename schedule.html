Overview

Wedding celebrations will take place over two days, including a welcome ceilidh on Friday the 7th of November for those who can make it. This will be followed by our wedding and reception on Saturday the 8th of November. Please fill out the RSVP form to let us know if you plan on making both as well as your dietary requirements and any other specifics.

Transportation to the Church and then the reception venue on the day of the wedding is available via coach and will be departing from the town of Beauly. Just let us know if you'd like to reserve a seat!

Welcome Ceilidh

Friday, 7th of November
Moniack Castle, IV5 7PQ
6:30 PM - 10 PM
Dress Code: Casual
Open to everyone but attendance very much optional. This is an opportunity to learn some reels/ceilidh dancing and meet other guests. As a traditional ceilidh, there will be an open floor for guests to contribute words, music or any special talents!

Wedding Ceremony

Saturday, 8th of November
St. Mary's Church, Eskadale, IV4 4PJ
2 PM - 3:30 PM
Dress Code: Unapologetic Glamour! Black Tie/Tartan encouraged.
The church might be cold so wrap up warm. Parking will be available in an adjacent field. There will also be a bus service available from Beauly to the Church and then to the reception afterwards.

Reception

Saturday 8th of November
Moniack Castle, IV5 7PQ
4 PM - Late
Dress Code: Unapologetic glamour — Tartan, velvet, silks and satin encouraged. Don’t be shy.
The reception will be in the grounds of Moniack Castle. Kilts are very much encouraged and make sure you wear shoes you can dance in! Parking will be available in an adjacent field. For those who aren’t driving, a late-night coach service will be available back to Beauly for those interested. You can also arrange a pre-booked taxi at one of the listed taxi services in travel and accommodation.
