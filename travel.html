Travel & Accommodation

Travel

If you're arriving from out of town, we recommend flying or taking the train to Inverness. If you're coming from London, there are regular flights from Heathrow, Gatwick and Luton. If you're coming from the United States, it can sometimes be cheaper to fly to Amsterdam and then get a direct flight to Inverness. All events are within a half-hour drive of the city.

If you'd like to rent a car, you can organise that at the Inverness airport or with Jack's Car hire in Inverness, who offer great deals.

Accommodation

We recommend staying in Beauly, which is a 30-minute drive from Inverness airport and is close to all wedding venues. If you have a car though, there are other options further afield around Loch Ness, Inverness and elsewhere.
