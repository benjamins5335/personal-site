Travel & Accommodation

Travel

If you're arriving from out of town, we recommend flying or taking the train to Inverness. If you're coming from London, there are regular flights from Heathrow, Gatwick and Luton. If you're coming from the United States, it can sometimes be cheaper to fly to Amsterdam and then get a direct flight to Inverness. All events are within a half-hour drive of the city. There is a train station in Beauly.

If you'd like to rent a car, you can organise that at the Inverness airport or with Jack's Car hire in Inverness, who offer great deals. If you are staying in Beauly renting a car shouldnâ€™t be necessary as there are lots of taxi companies in the inverness area.

Accommodation

We recommend staying in Beauly, which is a 30-minute drive from Inverness airport (1 hour on the train) and is close to all wedding venues. If you have a car, there are other options further afield around Loch Ness, Inverness and elsewhere.

Hotels in Beauly

Lovat Arms
Smack bang in the middle of Beauly with its own adjoining pub, this is a Fraser-themed hotel with lots of character. This is the best there is in terms of convenience and location.
The Priory Hotel
Also in Beauly, a highly convenient 3-star hotel.
Downright Gabbler
A nice B&B also in Beauly.

Hotels Further Afield

Loch Ness Country House Hotel
A nice 4 star hotel close to Inverness with a golf course.
Estate of Ord
Rustic family-owned hotel a few miles north of Beauly right next door to a distillery.
Bunchrew House Hotel
A beautiful 4-star hotel with spectacular views of the Moray Firth.
